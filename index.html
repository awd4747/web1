Make my own mechanical keyboard

Generally, the types of keyboards distributed on the market are fixed at 87 keys, 104 keys, and 108 keys.
With the recent trend of custom keyboards, various unique arrangements have been released, but it takes a long time to receive them after payment, or most of them are very expensive prices.

And even some people want to make weird arrangement of keys.

키보드의 종류

  1. 멤브레인

  2. 플린저

  3. 무접점
